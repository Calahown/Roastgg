FAQ - General

What is roast.gg?

Roast.gg is a VOD review marketplace. It is designed to help competitve players who are looking to improve their gameplay with high ranking players.
The high ranking players sell personalized advice based on a gameplay video.

How do I record gameplay?

Keep in mind these are recomendations. You are welcome to use any service you want.
For Windows 10 users:
  Pressing windowskey + g opens the gamebar which will let you record.
    The gamebar is part of the Xbox app. By default the maximum video length is set to 1 hour.
    Some games take longer, so it might be good to change it.
For other Windows versions:
Plays.tv app allows for easy gameplay recording. The default video quality settings may be a little low.
   For more information: www.plays.tv
For Mac users:
Quicktime is a good option. Please follow the guide below. It is not necessary to use the
   higher framerate part of the guide, but it might be desirable.
   Quicktime guide: https://www.imore.com/how-screen-record-gameplay-mac

FAQ - Buyer

How do I contact the seller before placing the order?

There is a contact me button in the gig description page, and in the user's profile page.

How do I upload my video?

Any video service is fine. We recommend Youtube unlisted video.
Please note that the seller may have specific instructions for video delivery.

How do I pick a seller?

We recommend a seller that is not too high above your current rank.
The meta game is different at different ranks, and the amount of notes during the review could be overwhelming. This can make it hard to focus.
Pick a seller that mains the role you do.

How do I order a gig?

Click on the gig windows until you find one that you like, payment methods are displayed on the right side.
After selecting the gig you want to purchase, and clicking the purchase button. You will be brought to a transaction screen.
In this screen you will be able to chat with the seller for particular inquiries about format and other specifics.
In general you will have recorded a VOD you want reviewed and send a link to it through the chat.
After the seller delivers the review you may use the chat to clarify anything, but the transaction is considered complete and you may review your seller.

FAQ - Seller

How do I get paid?

Roast.gg utilizes Stripe to make payments. We ask that sellers create a Stripe account before posting their first gig.
When an order for your gig is recieved the payment should be displayed in your Stripe account.
Roast.gg keeps 10% + $0.30 usd of each sale to cover Stripe fees. This rate may change after the beta.
The payments automatically rollout from your Stripe account to your bank account.
It is possible to change the rollout intervals inside your Stripe account by navigating to the Stripe Dashboard > clicking on payouts > Settings.

Why Stripe?

We believe Stripe is our best choice to keep transaction cost down. We pass on the savings on to you.

What about chargebacks?

Chargebacks, like taxes are just a reality of life. At Roast.gg we have opted to keep our fees low at 7% + $0.30.

During the beta:
As a seller, you are responsible for your buyer's chargebacks.
When a chargeback occurs, both Stripe and Roast.gg return their fees. It would be like the sale never happened.

This stance may change after beta. For now we at Roast.gg hope that the low fees make up for the lack of chargeback protection.
We would like to hear you out on this topic. Please do contact us at suggestions@roast.gg

Can I have a negative Stripe balance?

Yes, you can. It will most likely be as a result of a chargeback. For more information on what happens:
https://support.stripe.com/questions/what-happens-when-i-have-a-negative-balance
